Use-case (Melbourne House Price Analysis) -- Use-case
=========================================================================
You need to create a model that can predict the price of house based on Car Parking availability, Landsize, BuildingArea, YearBuilt. 

For that you are assigned as a Analyst and your job is to make the data compatible for Inferential stats.

Perform the following tasks:

1. Loading the data (pandas)
2. Check for missing data and repair the same







Perform above using both Stat approach and Domain approach. PFB the references for Domain approach

The usual  car parking alloted to each house as per govt policy is 1 
The Minimum build area for the building as per govt norms is 80
Maximums buildings as per the census survey was built in year 2000.
